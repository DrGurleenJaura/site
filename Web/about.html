About Us

Building education, technology and secure testing experiences with clarity and purpose.

DGx Creators blends IT solutions, EdTech expertise, AI workflows and a certified Pearson VUE Test Center — serving learners, institutions and organizations across Mohali, Chandigarh and beyond.

Our journey

From digital services to secure certification exams, DGx Creators is built to support education and technology with reliability.

Where it began

DGx Creators started as a small digital and IT support unit. Over time, we expanded into EdTech, AI workflows and accessibility projects — working with students, professionals and institutions.

Today, DGx Creators also operates as a certified Pearson VUE Test Center, delivering some of the most trusted global exams with secure setups and trained staff.

What drives us

We believe technology, education and reliability go hand-in-hand. Every project we handle — digital learning, IT setups, AI workflows, EdTech content or exam delivery — is executed with precision.

Our vision is to create a center where people can learn, test, build and grow — all in a trusted professional environment.

Our mission

To simplify access to secure testing, modern IT solutions and reliable EdTech support for individuals and organizations.

Our vision

A trusted center where technology, exams and digital learning operate together seamlessly — empowering careers and institutions.
